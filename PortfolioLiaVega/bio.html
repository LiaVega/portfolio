BIO

I am always discovering new things. Creativity can be applied to every aspect of our lives. I like astronomy, to paint staining myself and the small moments with great friends.

Throughout my life I have been interested in design, in how to tell things through images.

Already as a child I used to collect in my pockets objects that paired in colour.
